I'm Durjoy Morshed

Durjoy Morshed is a highly skilled and motivated individual with a expertise in web design & development with his background in apparel merchandising. He holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Apparel Merchandising, graduating in the top 95% of his class with an outstanding GPA of 3.98 out of 4.00. His academic brilliance earned him the prestigious Dean's Award, not once but an impressive four times throughout his educational journey.

**Professional Journey**
Durjoy's professional journey has been as diverse as his educational background. He began his career as a Marketing & Merchandiser at Olympic Fashion Ltd, where he honed his business acumen and keen eye for fashion trends. From there, he progressed to become a Trainee Merchandiser at M. M. Knitwear Ltd., where he learned the intricacies of garment manufacturing and merchandising.
With an unyielding passion for technology and design, Durjoy Morshed transitioned into the world of web design & development, where he has found his true calling. He has successfully undertaken various projects, showcasing his expertise in HTML, PHP, CSS, and JavaScript. From transforming designs from CUDA to HTML conversions, captivating College Websites, engaging E-commerce platforms, and Personalized Portfolio Websites.

**Notable Achievements & Projects**
Throughout his journey, Durjoy Morshed has excelled in numerous projects, including a project on Garment Knitwear, another on Industrial Engineering, and one on Merchandising of Knit Garments. His dedication and eye for detail have consistently impressed his peers and mentors. Beyond his technical skills, Durjoy possesses excellent interpersonal abilities, making him an outstanding negotiator and team leader. His knowledge of pattern making, sewing, cutting, and designing adds to his diverse skill set.

**Extra-Curricular Involvement**
Outside of his professional endeavors, Durjoy has been actively involved in various extra-curricular activities. He is a passionate volunteer for UNICEF Bangladesh, demonstrating his commitment to humanitarian causes. He is also part of the CREW at Uttara TV, where he actively contributes to media productions. As a former Treasurer of the BBC - Barajoyna Boys Club, Durjoy has proven his organizational and leadership skills. He has been an event organizer and led champion teams in cricket and football tournaments.

**Conclusion**
Durjoy Morshed is a multifaceted individual who seamlessly blends his expertise in web development with his background in apparel merchandising. His dedication, exceptional technical skills, and remarkable achievements make him a valuable asset to any project or team. With an insatiable thirst for knowledge and continuous growth, Durjoy is destined for even greater accomplishments in the future.
